# Phonotactics
- (C<sub>1</sub>)(H)(C<sub>1</sub>)V(C)(C) where `H = {y, w}` and `C₁ = {C - H}`
- Stress ==2nd==-to-last or ==elongated== syllable
- **x** ==cannot cluster==, and **w** can ==only cluster== in the ==middle of the onset==
